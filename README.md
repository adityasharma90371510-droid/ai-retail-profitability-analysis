AI-Assisted Retail Profitability Analysis
Business Problem

A retail company wants to identify:
profit drivers
loss-making products
the impact of discounting on profitability
Using transaction data, we analyze where the company makes money and where profit leakage occurs.

Dataset

Retail transaction dataset with 9,994 orders and 21 variables including:
Sales
Profit
Discount
Region
Category
Sub-category

Tools Used
Python (pandas)
Google Sheets dashboards
VS Code
AI-assisted analysis workflow

Key Insights

Furniture generates high sales but very low profit margins.
Tables and Bookcases are the largest loss-making products.
Discounts above ~30% consistently lead to negative profitability.
West and East regions generate the highest profit.

Strategic Recommendations

Reduce aggressive discount strategies
Reevaluate pricing for furniture products
Focus marketing on high-margin technology products
Expand successful product lines in West and East regions
